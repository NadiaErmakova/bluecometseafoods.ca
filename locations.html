Locations

Blue Comet Seafoods can be found throughout the Vancouver area of BC: the Steveston Fisherman’s Wharf and The Vancouver Farmer’s Market.
Find our location, dates and times listed below.

Our winter locations:

• Nat Bailey Farmers Market (Every Saturday, from Nov 7, 2015 to April 23, 2016)

• Hastings Park Farmers Market (Every Sunday, from Nov 8, 2015 to May 1, 2016)

Summer Farmer’s Markets:

• Trout Lake Farmer’s Market

• Kitsilano Farmer’s Market

• West End Farmer’s Market

• Main Street Farmers’ Market

• Yaletown Farmers Market



We also can be found at the Steveston Fisherman’s Wharf selling our frozen-at-sea Salmon off the Blue Comet.
