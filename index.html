Blue Comet Seafoods is a family run business with over 35 years of fishing experience. We supply wild, local, sustainable fish to our consumers.

Our products

All of our products are 100% wild and harvested from the pristine coastal waters of British Columbia. We provide sustai-nable seafood that is flash frozen at sea ensuring premium quality products. Our products are Ocean Wise® approved.

Oceana Reveals Mislabeling of America's Favorite Fish: Salmon

Oceana collected 82 salmon samples and found that 43 percent were mislabeled. DNA testing confirmed that most of the mislabeling (69 percent) consisted of farmed Atlantic salmon being sold as wild-caught product.
